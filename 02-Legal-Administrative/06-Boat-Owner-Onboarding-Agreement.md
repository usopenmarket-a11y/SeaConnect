# Boat Owner Onboarding Agreement - SeaConnect

**Purpose:** Terms and requirements for boat owners listing vessels on SeaConnect.

---

## Parties
- **Boat Owner (Provider):** [Name / Business Name]
- **Platform:** SeaConnect

---

## 1. Eligibility & Verification
- Boat owners must be 18+ and legally permitted to operate the vessel.
- Required documents for verification:
  - Vessel registration
  - Proof of ownership/authorization
  - Insurance certificate
  - Captain qualifications (if applicable)
  - Identification (government-issued ID)
- SeaConnect may perform background checks and KYC verification.

---

## 2. Listing Requirements
- Owners must provide accurate boat details: make, model, capacity, photos, safety equipment, amenities.
- Availability calendar must be kept up to date.
- Photo and video rights: Owner grants SeaConnect license to use listing media for marketing.

---

## 3. Pricing & Commission
- Owner will set pricing; SeaConnect will charge commission per booking as described in Vendor Agreement / Fee Schedule.
- Owners responsible for any taxes due on earnings.
- Owners must set cancellation terms consistent with platform policy.

---

## 4. Insurance & Safety
- Owners must maintain minimum insurance coverage as specified in platform insurance requirements.
- Owners must ensure safety equipment onboard and compliance with maritime laws.
- SeaConnect may request copies of insurance and safety documents at any time.

---

## 5. Handling Bookings
- Owners must respond to booking requests within [X] hours.
- Confirmed bookings create an agreement between Owner and Charterer per Boat Charter Agreement.
- Owners must honor confirmed bookings or provide reasonable alternatives.
- Repeated failures to honor bookings may result in penalties or removal from platform.

---

## 6. Payments & Payouts
- Owners will be paid per the settlement schedule (weekly/bi-weekly/monthly).
- Deductions apply for refunds, chargebacks, platform fees, and penalties.
- Owners must provide valid bank details for payouts.

---

## 7. Dispute Handling
- Owners agree to participate in SeaConnect dispute resolution processes.
- Owners must provide evidence (logs, photos) when disputes arise.
- Failure to cooperate may result in account suspension.

---

## 8. Termination & Suspension
- SeaConnect may suspend or terminate listings for safety violations, fraudulent activity, or repeated complaints.
- Owners may delist their vessel with [X] days' notice subject to pending bookings.

---

## 9. Liability & Indemnification
- Owners indemnify SeaConnect against claims arising from vessel operation, except where caused by SeaConnect's gross negligence.
- Liability limits should comply with local law.

---

## 10. Governing Law
- Governing law: [Egypt]. Disputes resolved in local courts or arbitration per agreement.
