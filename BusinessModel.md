⚓ Project Overview
App Name (example)

“SeaConnect” – Boats, Gear & Competitions in One App

Core Concept

A mobile + web platform that connects:

Boat & Yacht Owners → offer trips/fishing charters

Customers → book trips, buy fishing gear, and join competitions

Vendors → sell fishing equipment and accessories






| Section                    | Details                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| -------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Customer Segments**      | 1. Fishing enthusiasts<br>2. Tourists & families looking for sea trips<br>3. Boat/yacht owners<br>4. Vendors of fishing gear & accessories                                                 |
| **Value Proposition**      | - Easy booking for fishing/sea trips<br>- Central marketplace for fishing items<br>- Competitions to engage community<br>- Exposure for boat owners and vendors                            |
| **Channels**               | - Mobile app (Android/iOS)<br>- Website<br>- Social media marketing                                                                                                                        |

| **Revenue Streams**        | 1. Commission from trip bookings<br>2. Commission from marketplace sales<br>3. Vendor subscription fees<br>4. Featured ads (premium listings)<br>5. Competition entry fees or sponsorships |
| **Key Resources**          | - App development team<br>- Payment gateway<br>- Marketing & partnerships<br>- Cloud hosting & database<br>- Legal/business licenses                                                       |
| **Key Activities**         | - Develop & maintain app<br>- Onboard boat owners/vendors<br>- Manage marketplace logistics<br>- Organize fishing competitions                                                             |
| **Key Partners**           | - Boat/yacht associations<br>- Fishing gear suppliers<br>- Payment gateway providers<br>- Tourism boards or marinas                                                                        |
