## Revenue Streams Overview

SeaConnect has 5 primary revenue streams designed to create a sustainable, multi-sided marketplace. All rates below are final decisions for launch.

---

## Revenue Stream 1: Boat Charter Commissions

### Model
Platform takes 12% commission from each completed booking, charged to the boat owner. Fee is deducted automatically from the payout, which is released 24 hours after trip completion.

### Commission Structure
| Scenario | Rate | Notes |
|----------|------|-------|
| Standard booking | 12% | Applied to all completed bookings |
| Launch promotion (first 3 months) | 0% | To accelerate supply-side onboarding |
| Peak season (June–Sept, Eid periods) | 12% | No increase — volume compensates |
| Off-season | 12% | Stable rate; owner earns more per booking |

**Rationale for 12%:** Positioned below Airbnb Experiences (20%) and Viator (25%), but above local informal alternatives (0%). Competitive enough to attract owners while sustainable for the platform.

### Pricing Reference (Market Rates)
| Boat Type | Half-Day (≤6h) | Full-Day (6–10h) | Multi-Day (/day) |
|-----------|---------------|-----------------|-----------------|
| Small Fishing Boat (≤4 pax) | 800 EGP | 1,400 EGP | 1,000 EGP/day |
| Medium Fishing Boat (4–8 pax) | 1,400 EGP | 2,400 EGP | 1,800 EGP/day |
| Motor Yacht (6–10 pax) | 2,000 EGP | 3,500 EGP | 2,800 EGP/day |
| Large Yacht (10–20 pax) | 4,000 EGP | 7,000 EGP | 5,500 EGP/day |
| Luxury Charter (20+ pax) | 8,000 EGP | 15,000 EGP | 12,000 EGP/day |

### Year 1 Revenue Projection
| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Average booking value | 2,200 EGP |
| Commission per booking (12%) | 264 EGP |
| Month 1–3 bookings (ramp-up, 0% commission) | 0 EGP commission |
| Month 4–12 target bookings | 200/month |
| Monthly commission revenue (Month 4+) | ~52,800 EGP |
| **Year 1 Annual Total** | **~475,200 EGP** |

> Note: 0% commission months 1–3 means Year 1 commission is lower; full 12% from Month 4 onward.

## Revenue Stream 2: Marketplace Sales Commission

### Model
10% commission on every completed product sale, charged to the vendor. Deducted from payout, released 7 days after confirmed delivery (escrow period protects customers).

### Commission Rates by Category
| Product Category | Commission | Rationale |
|-----------------|------------|-----------|
| Rods & Reels | 10% | High value items, good margin |
| Lines, Hooks, Sinkers | 10% | High volume, low price; 10% still viable |
| Tackle Boxes & Storage | 10% | Medium volume, good margin |
| Fishing Apparel | 10% | Standard rate |
| Safety Equipment (PFDs) | 8% | Lower rate to encourage safety gear listings |
| Fish Finders & Electronics | 10% | High value, worth standard rate |
| Other Fishing Gear | 10% | Standard |

**Flat 10% simplifies vendor accounting** — no category-guessing or disputes.

### Year 1 Revenue Projection
| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Average order value | 480 EGP |
| Commission per order (10%) | 48 EGP |
| Month 1–3 orders | 50/month (ramp-up) |
| Month 4–12 orders | 150–200/month |
| **Year 1 Annual Total** | **~86,400 EGP** |

## Pricing Strategy

### Competitive Positioning
| Platform | Commission Rate | SeaConnect Position |
|---------|----------------|-------------------|
| Airbnb Experiences | 20% | We are 8pp cheaper for owners |
| GetYourGuide / Viator | 20–30% | We are 8–18pp cheaper |
| Etsy (marketplace) | 6.5% + listing fee | We are comparable (10%, no listing fee) |
| Amazon (marketplace) | 8–15% | We are competitive at 10% |

### Pricing Philosophy
1. **Value-based:** Owners should feel they earn more via SeaConnect than going it alone, even after commission
2. **Simplicity:** Flat rates across categories — no confusion, no negotiation
3. **Growth aligned:** Commission only on completed transactions — platform only earns when owners/vendors earn
4. **Transparent:** Pricing shown upfront in onboarding; no hidden fees

### Dynamic Pricing Considerations (Phase 2)
- Owner dashboard tool suggesting optimal pricing based on local demand, season, and competitor rates
- Platform does NOT auto-adjust prices — owners always approve
- A/B test higher commission rates (13–14%) in high-demand seasons when price elasticity drops

## Cost Structure (Platform Level)

### Cost of Revenue (COGS)
| Item | Rate |
|------|------|
| Fawry payment processing | 1.5% of transaction value |
| Stripe processing (Phase 2) | 2.9% + 1.00 EGP |
| SMS OTP (Twilio) | 0.45 EGP per SMS |
| Email (SendGrid) | ~0.002 EGP per email |
| **Estimated total COGS** | ~2.5% of GTV |

### Gross Margin Calculation
- Year 1 GTV (Gross Transaction Value): ~7,500,000 EGP
- Revenue take rate: ~12.2% of GTV
- COGS: ~2.5% of GTV → ~187,500 EGP
- **Gross Profit:** ~729,024 EGP
- **Gross Margin:** ~79.5%
