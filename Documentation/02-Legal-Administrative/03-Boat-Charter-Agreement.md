## 1. Definitions
- **Charter Period:** Start and end date/time of the charter.
- **Charter Fee:** Total fee payable by Charterer including platform commissions and taxes.
- **Deposit:** Amount required to secure booking.
- **Cancellation Window:** Time periods and refund percentages.

## 2. Booking & Payment
- Booking is confirmed once payment (deposit or full) is processed via SeaConnect.
- Payment schedule: Deposit [% or amount] due at booking; balance due [X] days before departure.
- Accepted payment methods: [Credit card, local e-payments, bank transfer].
- Platform fees and commissions are described in the Vendor Agreement / Fee Schedule.

## 3. Provider Obligations
- Maintain the vessel in seaworthy condition and compliant with all maritime regulations.
- Provide valid registration and insurance documents prior to first booking.
- Ensure qualified captain/skipper is present if required.
- Provide safety equipment as per local regulation and platform safety standards.
- Arrive at agreed embarkation point on time and ready to depart.
- Honor confirmed bookings or provide an acceptable alternative.

---

## 4. Charterer Obligations
- Provide accurate passenger manifest and any special requirements at booking.
- Pay charges in accordance with payment schedule.
- Comply with safety instructions given by the captain and provider.
- Not exceed vessel capacity.
- Be responsible for any damage caused by negligence or wilful misconduct.
